Description

An all-in-one platform to help the City in achieving its Local Government metric requirements, assist in the completion of sustainable development plans and support effective City Governance.

  • Customize

    • While essentially an off-the-shelf solution that can be deployed within a minimum amount of time, CityServ can be customized depending on the need of the City to address the its precise business requirements.

  • Cloud Based

    • Cloud-based service enhances functionality and is ready to deploy without expensive infrastructure costs.

  • Citizen Engagement

    • Effective connection & engagement of the Citizens’ demands and aspirations to responsible representative and political institutions.

Additional Information

CityServ is helping the local government innovate public service and affairs with city-specific content, providing citizen information, and industry platforms, public facilities data, and tourism rich content. CityServ's mission is to promote and better serve the local communities - for the people, by the people.

  • Citizen Reporting

    • Allows user to capture image using the phone to report incidents goes to email.

  • News

    • City News and City Announcements (push notification).

  • Directory

    • List of Government, NGO and Service establishments with contact details.

  • CCTV Live Viewing

    • Allows the User of the App to view the CCTV live on their devices.

  • Tourism Where to’s

    • Find out about special festivals and discover local events throughout the year. Or simply, get inspired for your next vacation.

  • Weather

    • Daily weather reports.

Quick Overview:

CityServ is a City Management Mobile & Web Application that can be customized for the Local Government’s objectives and business need.
